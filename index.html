ABOUT THE ARTIST

Rupert Ford, also known as TheHumanHeater, is a versatile designer from Albuquerque, New Mexico that specializes in Adobe Illustrator. He’s most well known for his graphic design and video editing skills, and he’s self-taught in Game and Web design. Rupert has 4 years of experience in game development and website design, around 10 years of experience in working props creation, 3 years of sound design, graphic design, and filmmaking, and 2 years of costume design.

"Creation is humanity’s greatest ability, it creates not only physical or digital art, but also other people and inspiration. And inspiration creates people that wish to create, creating a never ending cycle of creation and inspiration."
-Rupert Ford
